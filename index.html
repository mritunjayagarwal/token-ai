What is Trade AI?

Trade AI is a revolutionary, AI-driven platform that leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to predict market movements and assist traders in making judicious investment decisions. As the inaugural revenue-sharing AI-based platform, it enables traders to partake in the financial gains generated by the platform. Utilizing cutting-edge machine learning methodologies, it processes market data and furnishes traders with real-time market insights and predictions, thereby enabling them to make sound and lucrative trading choices.

Trade AI Ecosystem

Trai Token

Trai is the native cryptocurrency of the Trade AI ecosystem. It is an ERC-20 token based on the Ethereum blockchain. The token is used as a means of exchange within the ecosystem and can be used to access various services and features offered by the platform.

Grid Report

Grid Reports is an AI-generated market report feature that helps traders make informed decisions by providing them with verified insights and predictions about market movements in Trade AI ecosystem.

Trai Perp Dex

Trai Perp DEX is a decentralized exchange in Trade AI ecosystem that allows users to trade perpetual contracts for Bitcoin and other major cryptocurrencies directly, without the need for a central intermediary, thus providing a secure and transparent trading environment.

Trade Ai Academy

Trai Academy is a part of the Trade AI ecosystem that aims to educate the community about trading. It offers a range of free educational resources, including tutorials, webinars, and workshops, designed to help individuals learn about different trading strategies, market analysis, and risk management.

Grid Report

The TRAI BTC Range Report is a robust machine learning tool that utilizes predictive capabilities to aid the crypto trading community.

Every Tuesday at 9 AM EST it furnishes a 7-day forecast of predictions for Bitcoin's price and price range. While the TRAI BTC Range Report is not a form of financial advice, it can be an effective tool that facilitates more knowledgeable and less emotionally driven choices in crypto trading.
